Hobbies

Hobbies and Interests

Outside of academics and professional pursuits, I am passionate about staying active and embracing the great outdoors. I thoroughly enjoy a wide range of outdoor activities that challenge me both physically and mentally. Mountain biking is one of my favorite ways to explore nature while pushing my limits on various trails. Skiing and snowboarding offer a thrilling way to enjoy the winter months, with the crisp air and snow-covered mountains providing the perfect backdrop for adventure. Boating is another activity I cherish, as it allows me to relax on the water while enjoying time with family and friends. Additionally, basketball has always been a staple in my life, giving me an outlet for friendly competition and teamwork.

Beyond outdoor activities, I find immense joy in creative and social hobbies. Playing the guitar is one of my favorite ways to unwind and express myself, whether I’m learning new songs or creating my own melodies. Music has always been a source of inspiration and relaxation for me. I also enjoy spending time with friends and family playing card games. Whether it’s a casual round of a classic game or learning a new strategy-based one, card games are a fun way to connect, strategize, and share laughter.

These hobbies not only reflect my love for adventure and creativity but also highlight my values of teamwork, connection, and continuous learning. They are a vital part of who I am and help me maintain a balanced, well-rounded lifestyle.
